User Research

The target users of this project were seniors (+65) who are lonely and are sedentary. Home care, care home, rehabilitation facilities and hospitals are settings that these users belong. The first step was to visit them in a weekly exercise where they perform a shared cooking activity.

After the inverviews, all information was clustered with an Affinity Diagram. From the AD, interesting insights about seniors view on different subjects, such as healthy eating, cooking, technology acceptance and socialization.

Ideation

To continue the design process after the user research, the group decided to use the design sprint methodolog. A problem statement and long term goal were defined. We've mapped the problem in order to understand the relationship of different actors during different stages of the process and as a result we got a couple of possible approachs for the project.
