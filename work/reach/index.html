Challenge

Design a digital prototype that

facilitates food-related activities

for a specific group of seniors.

What is it about?

Reach 2020 is an European Consortium that aims to help elderly by focusing on stimulating healthy eating among seniors. The stakeholders of the projects requested my team to research a particular group of seniors in Eindhoven. These seniors participates (takes part?) in a weekly cooking activity with students of the Summa College, as part of the Lokaal+ initiative. Further more, the stakeholders asked my team to develop a digital prototype for a smart table that would engage seniors in food-related activities. The interactive table, built by researchers of the Technical University of München, could be used in a On this project, we worked for the European Consortium Reach 2020, in collaboration with the TU München and the TU Eindhoven..

What did we do?

During four weeks we: interviewed seniors during a cooking activity; clustered qualitative data from the contextual inquiry using affinity diagram; answered business questions by following the design sprint method; prototyped a cooking-aid using an interactive table, provided by TU München; conducted usability tests with 8 users; ran three iterations of the design process.

What did we achieve by the end of the project?

At the end of the case, we provided a prototype to our stakeholders so that they can continue the next stage of the project. Reach Consortium have a prototype that can be applied in their context of use, like the Lokaal+ activity for instance. From TU München side they were interested in the design implications that we learned by observing seniors using De Kooktafel during cooking activities.
