				<h1 class="service-heading" style="font-weight: normal; padding-top: 60px; font-size:3em; ">What is it <b>about</b>?</h1>
				<p>
					 Reach 2020 is an European Consortium that aims to help elderly by focusing on stimulating healthy eating among seniors.
					 The stakeholders of the projects requested my team to research a particular group of seniors in Eindhoven. These seniors participates (takes part?) in a weekly cooking activity with students of the Summa College, as part of the Lokaal+ initiative. Further more, the stakeholders asked my team to develop a digital prototype for a smart table that would engage seniors in food-related activities. The interactive table, built by researchers of the Technical University of München, could be used in a 
					 On this project, we worked for the European Consortium <b>Reach 2020</b>, in collaboration with the TU München and the TU Eindhoven..
				</p>

		<!--What did we do?-->
		<div class="row" style="padding-top: 30px">
			<div class="col-lg-8 col-lg-offset-2 text-left">
				<h1 class="service-heading" style="font-weight: normal; padding-top: 60px; font-size:3em; ">What did <b>we do</b>?</h1>
				<p>	During four weeks we:
					<b>interviewed</b> seniors during a cooking activity;
					clustered <b>qualitative data</b> from the contextual inquiry using <b>affinity diagram</b>;
					answered business questions by following the <b>design sprint</b> method;
					<b>prototyped</b> a cooking-aid using an interactive table, provided by TU München;
					conducted <b>usability tests</b> with 8 users;
					ran three <b>iterations</b> of the design process.
				</p>
