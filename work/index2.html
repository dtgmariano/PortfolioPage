Who am I?

I am a User Experience Designer based in Eindhoven, The Netherlands, currently working within the User System Interaction program at TU/e. As an addition to my UX skills, I have a Biomedical and Electrical Engineering background.

My vision

As a UX designer, I am inspired to explore new ideas that can empower people's life. With a user-centered approach, my purpose is to meet users needs with business goals, to design a successful product.
