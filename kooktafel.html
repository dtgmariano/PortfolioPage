Challenge

Design a digital prototype

that facilitates food-related activities

for a specific group of seniors.

What is it about?

On this project, we worked for the European Consortium Reach 2020, in collaboration with the TU M端nchen and the TU Eindhoven. Reach 2020 aims to help elderly by focusing on stimulating healthy eating among seniors.

What did we do?

During four weeks we: interviewed seniors during a cooking activity; clustered qualitative data from the contextual inquiry using affinity diagram; answered business questions by following the design sprint method; prototyped a cooking-aid using an interactive table, provided by TU M端nchen; conducted usability tests with 8 users; ran three iterations of the design process.

What did we achieved by the end of the project?

At the end of the case, we provided a prototype to our stakeholders so that they can continue the next stage of the project. Reach Consortium have a prototype that can be applied in their context of use, like the Lokaal+ activity for instance. From TU M端nchen side they were interested in the design implications that we learned by observing seniors using De Kooktafel during cooking activities.
