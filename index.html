About me

I'm a User Experience Designer based in Eindhoven, The Netherlands, currently working within the User System Interaction program at TU/e.

For the past four years, I've been designing assistive technologies solutions for people with communication disorders. I'm inspired to create new solutions that can empower people's life.

To support my skills as a UX professional, I have a background in Biomedical and Electrical Engineering!
