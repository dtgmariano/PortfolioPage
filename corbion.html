ABOUT THE CLIENT

Corbion is the global market leader in lactic acid, lactic acid derivatives, and a leading company in emulsifiers, functional enzyme blends, minerals, vitamins and algae ingredients. Their factory in Gorinchem runs various process line 24/7. A central control room monitors all processes in that facility. As part of the "Control Room of the Future" Project, Corbion is addressing new features for its CR.
