Concept

A customizable dashboard where meaningful data can easily be compared and where the user is frequently updated.

Briefing

Eventix is an online e-ticket solution for event organizers all around the world. Their customers are festivals, theaters, business and sports organizers. One of their products is the desktop dashboard. The dashboard provides to organizers information about sales, insights from marketing, demographics about the audience, etc. These data are fundamental to support business decisions. Therefore, their customers should have a great experience while using this feature. Eventix asked my team to design a dashboard with a better experience for customers that would also reduce the need for supports during and after the sales stage.

During the initial weeks, my team mates and I performed several key activities: Heuristic evaluation of the product, competitors analysis, remote interview with users and on-line surveys. The team gathered a list of requirements necessary to enhance the sales dashboard. Next, throughout two iteration cycles we did brainstorming sessions, built lo-fi and hi-fi prototypes and conducted user tests.
