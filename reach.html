BRIEFING

ABOUT THE CLIENT AND THEIR PROBLEM

Reach 2020 is an European Consortium that aims to help elderly by focusing on stimulating healthy eating among seniors. The stakeholders of the projects requested my team to research a particular group of seniors in Eindhoven. These seniors participate in a weekly cooking activity at the Summa College. Further more, the stakeholders asked my team to develop a digital prototype for a smart table that would aid seniors to cook and socialize.
